<p><strong>Description :</strong>
</p>
<p>The purpose of our CCA is to engage and train students in the sport of
Wushu to build their core strength, agility and mastery of the skill. To
achieve this, weekly training is conducted for our members. For those who
show more interest and flair in the sport, further training is provided
during school team practices. These students also perform for the school
on special occasions and represent us in competitions.</p>
<p><strong>Goals:&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p>To build up students’ health, confidence and self-discipline through the
sport of Wushu.&nbsp;</p>

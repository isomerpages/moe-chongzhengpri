<p>Greetings and welcome to Chongzheng Primary School’s website! This is
our official presence online and is updated regularly to inform and share
essential information on our school.</p>
<p>To our parents, thank you once again for entrusting your children with
us, as we aim to create the&nbsp;<strong>Best Day Ever</strong>&nbsp;experience
for all the students that pass through our gates.</p>
<p>After the fanfare of our 40<sup>th</sup> anniversary commemoration last
year, we are ready to embark on <strong>a new journey</strong> for the school,
to move towards a new era as we continue to serve the Tampines community.
As always, when it comes to developing and nurturing our students, we look
to the dedication of our staff (both teaching and non-teaching), as well
as support from the respective stakeholders (School Advisory Committee,
Parent Support Group – PaVE, parents, alumni, and our partners).</p>
<p>We seek guidance from MOE’s overall directions and implement programmes
that cater to the needs and interests of this group of 21<sup>st</sup> century
learners. Anchoring on the&nbsp;<strong>enhanced 21CC framework</strong>&nbsp;for
schools as well as leveraging emerging&nbsp;<strong>EdTech</strong>&nbsp;tools
such as AI, we ideate and realise ways to enhance the students’ learning
experiences and in the process also inculcate values that build an innovative
thinker and exemplary leader.</p>
<p>We believe that&nbsp;<strong>every child is a flower waiting to blossom</strong>&nbsp;here
in our school. We would like our students to navigate the complexities
and intricacies of this ambiguous but exciting 21<sup>st</sup> century world
with a spirit of curiosity and courage. We recognise the unique talents
and dreams that lie within every child. In partnership with parents, we
provide the nourishment and sunlight needed for every child to bloom. Throughout
the process, we keep our school Motto, "<strong>Confident and Steadfast</strong>",
close to our hearts.</p>
<p>Do feel free to explore our website further and discover the vibrant world
of Chongzheng Primary School. We are excited to welcome you to our community
and partner with you on this journey of learning and growth.</p>
<p>Join us for an exciting 2026!</p>

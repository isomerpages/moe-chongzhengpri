<p><strong>Cyberwellness Fest: Empowering Safe, Smart, and Responsible Digital Citizens</strong>
</p>
<p>In today’s digitally connected world, our students are growing up surrounded
by technology that shapes how they learn, communicate, and interact. To
support them in navigating this landscape safely, confidently and responsibly,
our school organizes the <strong>Annual Cyberwellness Fest</strong> – an
event to building strong cyberwellness habits in a fun and engaging way.
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>Why Cyberwellness Matters More Than Ever</strong>
</p>
<p>With increased access to devices, online platforms, and social media,
students are exposed to both opportunities and risks in the digital space.
Issues such as cyberbullying, online scams, misinformation, and excessive
screen time highlight the urgent need for students to develop sound judgment
and responsible online behaviours.</p>
<p>The Cyberwellness Fest plays a crucial part in equipping students with
the knowledge, values, and skills to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Stay safe online&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Make informed digital choices&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstrate respect and empathy in online environments&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build a positive digital footprint&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>By fostering these competencies early, we empower our students to thrive
as responsible digital citizens in an increasingly complex world.</p>
<p><strong>Integrated Learning with Purpose</strong>
</p>
<p>The Cyberwellness Fest is thoughtfully designed to complement and reinforce
learning from both the <strong>Form Teacher Guidance Period (FTGP)</strong> and
the school’s <strong>Cyberwellness curriculum</strong>. Rather than being
a standalone event, it serves as a meaningful extension of classroom learning,
allowing students to apply what they have learnt in authentic and engaging
contexts.</p>
<p>Through this integration, key cyberwellness messages are revisited, deepened,
and contextualised—ensuring that learning is not only understood but also
internalised.
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>Experiential Learning at the Heart</strong>
</p>
<p>At the core of the Cyberwellness Fest is <strong>experiential learning</strong>—students
learn best when they are actively involved. The fest features a variety
of interactive and hands-on activities such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Scenario-based games and simulations&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Role-playing exercises to practise decision-making&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Digital quizzes and challenges&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaborative problem-solving tasks&nbsp;</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>These activities are designed to place students in realistic online situations,
encouraging them to think critically, reflect on their actions, and make
responsible choices.
<br>
</p>
<p><strong>Building Future-Ready Learners</strong>
</p>
<p>The Cyberwellness Fest and the pre and post-programmes go beyond awareness—it
aims to shape attitudes and behaviours. By engaging students in meaningful
experiences, the fest nurtures:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Critical thinking and discernment in digital consumption&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Social-emotional competencies such as empathy and self-awareness&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Confidence in managing online interactions safely&nbsp;
<br>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>As technology continues to evolve, so too must our efforts in guiding
students. The Cyberwellness Fest represents our school’s commitment to
preparing students not just for academic success, but for life in a digital
world.</p>
<p>Together, we can create a safe and positive cyberspace for every child.
<br>
</p>

<p>The aims of art education in Singapore schools are to enable every child to enjoy art, communicate visually, and make meaning through connecting with society and culture.</p>
<p>The CZPS Art department aims to develop students&rsquo; confidence and skills to express themselves in varied modes by creating maximum participation for all. It also encourages imagination and cultivates in students the capacity to innovate and improvise. Platforms such as internal and external competitions are provided to every child to showcase and nurture their talents.</p>
<p><strong>CZPS Art Dept events/programme</strong></p>
<p>P1: Digital Art on MS Paint<br />P2: Digital Art on MS PowerPoint<br />P3: Digital Art on Powerpoint, Print making &amp; Art Pass programme<br />P4: Digital Art on Photoshop (Poster Making), &amp; Metal Tooling<br />P5: Digital Art on Photoshop, Photography &amp; Clay Making<br />P6: Digital Art on Photoshop, Photography &amp; Silk Screen Printing</p>
<p>All: Progressive art skills, Art Appreciation, Integration of Art in NE, Music, HE &amp; Environmental Awareness</p>
